About Me

A personal profile built around curiosity, atmosphere, and clear thinking.

I enjoy experiences that are quiet but memorable. Independent games and logic puzzles appeal to me because they combine mood, structure, and discovery in a way that feels personal and meaningful.

Who I Am

Zou Haoming

I am a student creating a personal website that reflects both who I am and how I think. I prefer a calm and organized style, and I like work that feels sincere rather than exaggerated.

Why Games Matter To Me

Meaning beyond entertainment

I like independent games because they often communicate strong ideas through art direction, atmosphere, and subtle storytelling. I like puzzle games because they reward patience, attention, and the ability to connect small details.

What I Value

Observation, taste, and structure

My interests have shaped the way I look at design. I appreciate clarity, rhythm, and balance, and I enjoy experiences that feel carefully built from the inside out.

My Perspective

How games connect to my personality

The games I enjoy most are not always the loudest ones. I am more interested in works that build tension through atmosphere, tell stories through details, and create a sense of discovery through careful design.

That preference also affects how I present myself. I want this website to feel simple, modern, and quietly expressive, like a small portfolio with its own mood.
